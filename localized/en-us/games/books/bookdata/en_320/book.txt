Come Fishing


Simon and George like to fish. 
They walk to the pond.Simon says,
“Come fishing, Ruth.”Ruth does not want to fish.She tries to stop the boys. 
She doesn’t want them to hurt the fish.Ruth worries about the fish. 
Will the boys catch them?Simon feels a tug. 
George feels a tug, too.They try to land the fish. The fishing rods break.The fish tricked the boys!
