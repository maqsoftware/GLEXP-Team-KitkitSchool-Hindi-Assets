Lost Crocodile


A crocodile and her children were playing.They decided to swim together.It got dark. 
But one child swam away from her family. She kept swimming and swimming.It got very dark. 
She lost her family.She met a dolphin. 
She asked,
“Have you seen my family?” 
The dolphin said,
“No.”She met a snail. 
She asked,
“Have you seen my family?”  
The snail said,
“No.”

Then she heard a noise. She found her family! She sat on her mom. 
She likes her mom’s nose.She was happy to find her family.
