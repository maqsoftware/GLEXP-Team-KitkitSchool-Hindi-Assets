Tasty Foods


Different kinds of foods can be tasty.Fruits can be sweet. 
They can be full of vitamins.Vegetables can be crunchy!Meat is chewy. 
It can make you strong.Rice is a grain. 
Grains are small seeds that come from plants.Milk is good for your bones. Cheese is made from milk. 
Bread is made from grains.Always drink lots of water.
