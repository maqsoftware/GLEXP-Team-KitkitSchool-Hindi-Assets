Up and Down


Lakshman climbs down the stairs.


He goes to the playground. 
He can climb up the slide!


Lakshman can climb up the stairs to their house.


Lakshman can climb up on the chair to get his car.


Lakshman can climb up onto the table.


But how will he climb down?


His father finds Lakshman on the table. 
He says, 
“Be careful where you climb.”


Lakshman slowly climbs down from the table.


Lakshman can climb up and down!
