Some Fruits Grow on Trees


Some fruit grow on trees. 
Oranges grow on trees. Oranges ripen in the winter. Pomegranates are red. 
They are full of seeds that taste sweet.Papayas are also filled with seeds.Jackfruits have prickly skin.Bananas grow in bunches!Guavas are green. 
Some guavas are pink too.  Coconuts have a green shell that is hard to break! Mangoes can be orange, yellow, red, or green!
