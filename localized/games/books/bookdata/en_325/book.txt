Little Painters


Sister and brother find some paint.Green paint. 
Blue paint. 
Yellow paint. 
Red paint.Dad and uncle paint the house.Sister asks to help.Dad says to paint the gate.Paint it green. 
Paint it red.Time to clean up!Wash your hands. 
Wash your face.Sister says the gate is red!Brother says the gate is green!Mother says the gate is red and green! 
Good painting, children!
