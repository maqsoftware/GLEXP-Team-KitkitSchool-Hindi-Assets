Little Sock Finds Home


All of the Socks got washed today. Then Little Sock falls out of the basket! 
Little Sock gets left behind!Little Sock needs help getting home. “Will you help me get home, Robot?”
Little Sock asks.“Yes,”
says Robot. 
“You can take my car.”“Will you help me get home, Spider?”
Little Sock asks.“Yes,”
says Spider. 
“Follow this marble home.”Little Sock follows the marble.The marble takes Little Sock home!Little Sock is happy to be back with its family.
